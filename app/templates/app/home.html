{# ── About us ── #}

MORE ABOUT US

Who we are

Cubic3D is a professional 3D printing service based in Nairobi, Kenya. We exist to make advanced manufacturing accessible — not just to large organizations, but to individuals, students, and small businesses too.

How it works

Upload your 3D file, choose your material and color, and we send you a confirmed price. Give us at least 48 hours and your part is ready. We deliver countrywide, and payment is only required before dispatch.

Our commitment to quality

Every print is tuned for dimensional accuracy — down to 0.02 mm in some cases. We don't just hit print and hand it over; we inspect each part so it arrives true to your design, with no further finishing needed.

A personalized service

No two print requests are the same. We take the time to understand your requirements so the finished part is exactly what you had in mind — whether that's an architectural model, a custom enclosure, or a one-off prototype.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We accept STL files, which is the standard format exported by most CAD tools including Fusion 360, SolidWorks, Tinkercad, and Blender. If your design is in a different format, reach out before uploading and we'll help you convert it.

Most orders are ready within 2–5 business days after you confirm the price. Larger or more complex prints may take longer, and we'll give you a specific timeline once we've reviewed your file.

Pricing is based primarily on the weight of the finished print (KES per gram), which depends on the size and density of your model. The material you choose also affects the cost. Upload your file to get an instant estimate before committing.

We currently offer PLA, PETG, ASA, ABS, TPU, and more, each available in a range of colors. PLA is great for most everyday objects, PETG for functional parts, and TPU for flexible applications. Visit our Materials page for a full breakdown.

Yes — we deliver countrywide across Kenya. Delivery fees are calculated at the invoicing stage based on your location. Payment is only required after you confirm the price and before dispatch.

Absolutely. We offer a 3D design service where we model your idea from a sketch, photo, or description — starting from KES 1,500. We also have a 3D scanning service to digitize an existing physical object. Get in touch to discuss your project.

We inspect every print before dispatch. If a part fails to meet the expected quality due to a printing error on our end, we reprint it at no extra charge. Issues arising from problems with the original 3D file — such as non-manifold geometry — will be discussed with you before we proceed.
