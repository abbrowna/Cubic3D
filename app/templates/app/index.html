What exactly is 3D printing?

Simply put, 3D printing, more technically known as Additive manufacturing, is the building of physical objects using a layer upon layer process.

Just as you may print letters or images onto a piece of paper, we can print an object into existance for you. You might even say we pull it right out of thin air!
