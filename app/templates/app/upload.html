Helpfull info

Here are some on the fly pointers that may help you decide. We however recommed that you visit the Materials page for more indepth explanations .

  1. PLA will work for most applications even mechanical in which case you should set the purpose field to mechanical
  2. If you really feel that your part will undergo some extra loads or has sections that will bend in order to join to other parts, then the flexibility and toughness of PETG should come in handy.
  3. Multicolor and Mechanical parts will generally be abit pricier.
  4. Colors can only be changed as the part transitions from one layer into another
  5. the designs you upload should be in .stl format. Most CAD software support exporting designs as .stl format. files obtained from Thingiverse are already in .stl format.
