How to use the re-fillable spools

1

  1. Distinguish between the inner and outer sides of the spool. The inner side is smooth while the outer side has grooves and a cutout for the label
  2. Align and insert each of the 3 short pieces with the corresponding slots on the inner side of the spool. They may be a bit tight.
  3. Place the filament re-fill into the partialy assembled spool.
  4. Align the other half of the spool and press to complete the assembly. Now you can cut the straps and pull them out.
